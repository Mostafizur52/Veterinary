Animal Health Tips

  • Feed your pet at fixed times and in proper quantity.
  • Ensure fresh and clean water is always available.
  • Bathe your animal at least once or twice a month to maintain hygiene.
  • Regular vaccinations are essential (e.g., Rabies, Parvo).
  • Clean their teeth and trim nails regularly.
  • Provide daily walks or physical activity for fitness.
  • Visit a vet for a full check-up at least once a year.
